# The Butler — member tile brief

## Subject
Dignified Butler portrait, head-and-shoulders emblematic. Poised older
gentleman, silver-and-slate hair swept back, trimmed gray mustache, calm
composed expression, eyes meeting the viewer with quiet authority. Black
tailcoat, white wing-collar shirt, black bow tie, white pocket square,
white gloves. Polished silver salver at lower edge. Matches sibling tiles
(the-architect.png, the-steward.png): classic bust, centered, slight
three-quarter view.

## Composition
- Square 1:1, bust, centered
- Ornate gilded dark-bronze border with vertical rivet rows, weathered patina
- Dark atmospheric haze background, depth matching sibling tiles

## Palette
- Slate #5b6472 dominant (tailcoat highlights, frame, haze)
- Warm ivory skin, white shirt/gloves/pocket square
- Cool steel silver salver
- Muted gold only as small frame highlights — slate is the signature
- Very dark slate / near-black ground

## Mood
Black-tie dignity, quiet competence, calm and slightly knowing.

## Style
Digital painting, fantasy trading-card finish, high detail on fabric,
gloves, hair, face. Soft rim lighting, painterly background.
