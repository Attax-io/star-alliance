# Butler Voice — skill tile brief

## Subject
A poised butler-herald delivering a plain spoken message. Centered bust,
three-quarter view, calm composed expression. One white-gloved hand is
raised gently, palm open, as if speaking plainly; from the open palm
flows a clean simple waveform ribbon of soft pale light — a single even
soundwave without complexity. An open scroll unrolls in the other hand
at waist level, plain parchment, no ornament. Conveys: clear plain voice,
not theatrics.

## Composition
- Square (1:1), centered subject, bust to mid-torso
- Ornate gilded border matching sibling skill tiles (obedience.png,
  ux-copywriting.png) — dark-bronze frame with small rivets
- Dark ground (near-black)
- Subtle floating embers behind, low intensity

## Palette
- Slate #5b6472 dominant (tailcoat, scarf, frame highlights)
- Cool steel grays on the waveform and scroll
- Warm ivory skin, white shirt and gloves
- Dark ground: very dark slate / near-black
- Muted, dignified, black-tie — no neon

## Mood
Quiet authority. Plain speech. Everything said will be understood the
first time.

## Style
Digital painting, fantasy trading-card finish, high detail on fabric and
skin, soft rim lighting, painterly atmospheric background.
