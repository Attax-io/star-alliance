# Helpless — skill tile brief

## Subject
A butler with empty white-gloved hands raised in a courteous "this is not
mine" gesture — palms up and slightly out, fingers relaxed, not defensive.
Hands are clearly empty. To one side, a sealed folded letter hovers just
past the frame, being passed onward to someone off-frame (only a sliver
of envelope and a gloved fingertip visible at the canvas edge). Conveys
polite refusal and redirection — the butler is routing the work, not
surrendering.

## Composition
- Square (1:1), centered subject, bust to mid-torso
- Both gloved hands raised near shoulder height, palms up
- One hand tilts slightly outward, gesturing to the off-frame recipient
- Ornate gilded border matching sibling skill tiles — dark-bronze frame
  with small rivets
- Dark ground (near-black)

## Palette
- Slate #5b6472 dominant (tailcoat, scarf, frame highlights)
- Cream / pale ivory for gloves, shirt cuffs, the sealed letter
- Cool steel grays on the wax seal
- Dark ground: very dark slate / near-black
- Dignified, restrained — black-tie courtesy

## Mood
Courteous deference. Composed redirection. Calm, self-possessed, no urgency.

## Style
Digital painting, fantasy trading-card finish, high detail on white gloves
and fabric folds, soft rim lighting, painterly atmospheric background.
