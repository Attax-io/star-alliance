# Scheduler Art Brief — homebrew.mxcl.ollama

Job id: `homebrew.mxcl.ollama`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A large clay amphora (terracotta, two-handled, narrow-necked) sits
centered on a stone hearth. The amphora's mouth is rimmed with warm
firelight and a small brass llama figure — stylized, geometric, with
distinctive long neck and small head — is leaping upward out of the
jar's mouth, mid-bound, all four hooves off the rim, casting a faint
golden shadow on the air beneath it. Below the hearth, glowing embers
pulse in a bed of ash. Faint curls of model-steam (warm orange vapor
shaped like tiny double-helix glyphs) rise around the llama.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, amphora centered on the hearth, llama leaping upward at
  the visual focal point
- Hearth stones frame the bottom third
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- A few floating embers scattered around the jar

## Palette
- Terracotta warm clay red-orange (amphora)
- Polished brass / honey gold (the llama)
- Ember red and orange (fire, embers)
- Dark hearth-stone gray
- Warm hearth-glow lighting throughout

## Mood
The local brew, alive and bounding. Self-hosted warmth.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
