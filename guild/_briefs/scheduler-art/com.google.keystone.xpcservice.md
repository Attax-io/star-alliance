# Scheduler Art Brief — com.google.keystone.xpcservice

Job id: `com.google.keystone.xpcservice`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
Two riveted iron pipes, slightly weathered and banded with brass rings,
crossing each other in an X at center. Each pipe carries a flowing
stream of glowing cross-process message-beads — small luminous spheres
of different colors racing along the pipe's length. Both pipes converge
on the SAME keystone-stone at upper right (the four-color quartered
shield from the agent tile), slotting into it from two different
angles. The keystone glows as messages arrive. A small gear-pulse
animation radiates from the keystone on each arrival — concentric
rings of soft light.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, X-crossed pipes dominate center, keystone at upper right
  as the destination
- Message-beads visible mid-flow along both pipes
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- Slight depth of field — keystone sharpest, far pipe-end slightly
  blurred

## Palette
- Dark iron with brass banding (pipes)
- Multi-colored message beads (mix of warm and cool tones)
- Four-color mark on the keystone (blue, red, yellow, green)
- Cool slate background, warm keystone glow
- Mechanical, purposeful, in-transit

## Mood
The hidden channel that keeps two halves of one system in sync.
Mechanical intimacy.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
