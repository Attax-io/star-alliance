# Scheduler Art Brief — lex-daily-bug-triage

Job id: `lex-daily-bug-triage`
Kind: native (Claude Routine)
Group: Claude Routines

## Subject
A single gauntleted hand (heavy plate armor, brass knuckles engraved with
runic sort-marks) is sorting a tipped wooden wheelbarrow full of stained,
crumpled scrolls into three wicker baskets on the ground below. The
baskets are unlabeled but distinguishable: one small and tidy (fixed),
one mid-size and orderly (triaged), one overflowing with smoke (open).
A single large moth — body shaped like a glowing ember-red beetle — is
lifting off from the wheelbarrow, wings trailing faint bug-shaped runes,
about to escape. The gauntlet fingers are mid-reach for it.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, the gauntlet hand and wheelbarrow dominate upper third,
  the three baskets ground the lower third
- Moth trails off to upper right, drawn by the eye
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- A few escaped smaller moths fluttering near the open basket

## Palette
- Cold steel and iron (gauntlet, wheelbarrow)
- Warm parchment cream and stained sepia (scrolls)
- Three baskets: muted green / blue / amber to distinguish
- Ember red (moth, bug runes)
- Moody, slightly chaotic — triage under pressure

## Mood
Dexterous, urgent, the daily sweep. One bug almost got away.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
