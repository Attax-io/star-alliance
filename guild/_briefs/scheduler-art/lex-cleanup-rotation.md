# Scheduler Art Brief — lex-cleanup-rotation

Job id: `lex-cleanup-rotation`
Kind: native (Claude Routine)
Group: Claude Routines

## Subject
Three leather-bound tomes standing upright on a polished brass pedestal
that is mid-rotation (the pedestal is a slow-turning lazy-Susan base
ringed with small gilt rivets). A comet-like streak of golden dust motes
spirals in from the upper right and wraps around the tomes, polishing
them — wherever the comet-trail touches, the leather turns brighter and
newly gilded, the spines regilded in fresh gold-leaf scrollwork. Faint
arcane runes shimmer in the dust-tail.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, three tomes centered on the rotating pedestal, slight
  three-quarter angle so all three are visible
- Comet streak enters upper right, sweeps down across the tomes,
  exits lower left
- Ornate gold runic border framing the tile, dark parchment
  background, pure black outer rim
- Small floating motes scattered in the empty corners

## Palette
- Warm oxblood and cordovan leather (tome covers)
- Bright brass / polished gold (pedestal, rivets, gilding, comet)
- Aged parchment cream (page edges, dust-mote glow)
- Deep shadow brown behind the pedestal
- Saturated, dramatic, candle-lit feel

## Mood
Meticulous, rejuvenating, the slow turning of the library wheel. Aged
made bright again.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
