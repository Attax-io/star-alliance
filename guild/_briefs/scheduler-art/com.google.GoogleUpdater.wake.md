# Scheduler Art Brief — com.google.GoogleUpdater.wake

Job id: `com.google.GoogleUpdater.wake`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A small anthropomorphic lamplighter figure (composed of a stout candle-
flame body and two thin wick-arms) is upending a four-color pinwheel
windmill — the pinwheel's four vanes are colored blue, red, yellow,
green (the four-color mark), turning in a lazy breeze. The lamplighter
is tipping the pinwheel toward a sleeping iron lantern hanging from a
bracket at right; the lantern is currently dark but as the pinwheel
turns over it, a fresh flame inside the lantern glass is reigniting —
first one wick-tip glows orange, then the whole mantle catches. A faint
trail of pinwheel motion-blur arcs across the frame.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, lamplighter and pinwheel centered-left, lantern at
  right
- Reigniting flame provides the focal warm light source
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- Pinwheel motion arc traces a smooth quarter-curve

## Palette
- Four-color mark: pure blue, red, yellow, green on the pinwheel
- Warm amber/orange (lamp flame, reignition)
- Iron black (lantern body, bracket)
- Dark parchment brown background
- Bright, hopeful, the daily wake-up

## Mood
Cheerful routine, the small reliable rekindling. Same task, same time,
always works.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
