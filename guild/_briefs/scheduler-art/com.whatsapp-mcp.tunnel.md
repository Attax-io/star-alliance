# Scheduler Art Brief — com.whatsapp-mcp.tunnel

Job id: `com.whatsapp-mcp.tunnel`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A torchlit stone tunnel bored straight through the heart of a mountain,
seen in true perspective from one end. The LEFT (head) opening of the
tunnel exits into a chat-cloud sky — fluffy green-tinted cloud-bubbles
drift outside in a bright sky. The RIGHT (tail) opening exits into an
interior device chamber — a small brass cogs-and-panels setup with a
glowing terminal screen. Along the tunnel walls, evenly spaced iron
torch-sconces burn with warm amber flame, lighting the stone. A
silhouetted figure mid-tunnel is carrying a message-envelope from left
to right.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, tunnel perspective from slight left-of-center, both
  openings visible at the frame edges
- Torches receding down both walls in diminishing scale
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- The silhouette is small but central, anchoring the eye

## Palette
- Cool stone gray (tunnel walls)
- Warm amber torchlight (flames, reflected highlights)
- Whatsapp green at the head opening (chat cloud)
- Brass and warm yellow at the tail opening (device)
- Strong contrast between the two ends, united by the tunnel

## Mood
The quiet passage through rock. Trusted subterranean channel.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
