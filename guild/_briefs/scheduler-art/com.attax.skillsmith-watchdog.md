# Scheduler Art Brief — com.attax.skillsmith-watchdog

Job id: `com.attax.skillsmith-watchdog`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A great bronze mastiff with two heads sitting guard at the iron door of
a vault. Its body is forged of polished bronze with riveted seams, a
heavy chain collar with a single gear-mark pendant. The LEFT head is
dozing — eyes closed, breath a thin curl of bronze steam, head tilted.
The RIGHT head is fully alert — ears up, eyes glowing amber, jaw
slightly open showing riveted teeth, gaze locked forward. The vault door
behind it is half-open, revealing racks of skill-blades glowing softly
in rows inside. A single bronze paw rests on the vault threshold,
holding the door from closing.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, mastiff centered in three-quarter view, vault door
  fills the background
- Two heads read clearly side by side
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- Subtle warm glow from inside the vault rimming the dog

## Palette
- Warm bronze with patina green (the mastiff)
- Glowing amber (alert eye, vault glow)
- Cool iron gray (vault door)
- Dark leather brown (shadowed floor)
- Vigilant, watchful, slightly mythic

## Mood
Constant vigilance, never sleeping entirely. The watcher that watches
while it sleeps.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
