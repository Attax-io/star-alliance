# Scheduler Art Brief — com.valvesoftware.steamclean

Job id: `com.valvesoftware.steamclean`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A great spinning brass cog at center, motion-blurred to suggest fast
rotation, with three steam-vent chimneys rising from its hub. White
condensation clouds billow from each vent — puffy cumulus bursts of
steam with bright golden sunlight catching their tops. At lower right,
a polished brass pressure-gauge is mounted, its dial face showing a
needle resting firmly in the GREEN band of the scale, two red zones
well clear on either side. The cog's teeth are engraved with small
gear-runes. A few stray droplets of condensation sparkle in mid-air.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, spinning cog centered, steam clouds rising into upper
  third
- Pressure gauge grounded at lower right
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- Subtle motion-lines around the cog rim

## Palette
- Polished brass / honey-gold (cog, gauge, vents)
- White steam with warm sunlit gold on the cloud tops
- Gauge green (the safe reading), pale cream dial, red warning zones
- Deep industrial brown / black behind
- Satisfying, well-maintained, the clean running of the system

## Mood
Clean power, healthy pressure. The system running smooth.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
