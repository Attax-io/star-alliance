# Scheduler Art Brief — com.google.keystone.agent

Job id: `com.google.keystone.agent`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A massive cathedral arch in pale stone, the keystone slot at its crown
occupied by a single carved keystone-stone. The keystone is carved with
the four-color arch mark (blue, red, yellow, green quadrant segments in
a quartered shield) and fitted perfectly into place, the surrounding
masonry tightly fitted around it. A mosaic of small colorful glass
tesserae fans out from the keystone down across the arch's inner curve
— radiant stained-glass light spills through it, casting a kaleidoscope
of four-color shafts onto the floor below. The arch is complete and
bearing weight.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, full arch centered, keystone at top-center as the
  visual focal point
- Light rays from the mosaic fan down to lower third
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- Cathedral floor in shadow at the very bottom

## Palette
- Pale limestone / cream (arch stones)
- Vivid four-color mark (keystone shield) — blue, red, yellow, green
- Stained-glass glow in the same four colors, slightly desaturated
- Warm gold light (border, edges)
- Reverent, monumental, foundation-stone energy

## Mood
Foundational, the piece that holds everything else up. Quietly
essential.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
