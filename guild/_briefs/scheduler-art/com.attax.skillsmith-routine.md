# Scheduler Art Brief — com.attax.skillsmith-routine

Job id: `com.attax.skillsmith-routine`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A dwarven forge-anvil at center, on which a glowing skill-blade is being
reforged — the blade is laid flat, glowing molten orange along its edge,
and a great smith's hammer is mid-strike above it. From every hammer-blow,
sparks of bright forge-fire rise in slow arcs and, as they cool mid-air,
harden into distinct rune-shaped stars — five-pointed golden glyphs of
different shapes drifting up like fireflies into the dark space above.
A glowing rune on the blade's fuller (a gear-mark) marks it as a
skill-blade, not a weapon.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, anvil and blade dominate the lower half
- Hammer caught mid-swing at upper-center, sparks rising upward
- Floating rune-stars fill the upper third
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- Small forge-bellows visible at left edge

## Palette
- Molten orange and forge-yellow (blade, sparks)
- Cool iron gray and dark steel (anvil, hammer)
- Gold (rune-stars, border)
- Deep forge-shadow black behind
- Heat-lit, dramatic

## Mood
Disciplined craft. The skill re-forged on schedule.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
