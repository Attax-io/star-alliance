# Scheduler Art Brief — com.whatsapp-mcp.server

Job id: `com.whatsapp-mcp.server`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A brass message-dispatcher desk, polished to a warm honey sheen, lit by
two parchment-shaded lamps on tall thin stands — each lamp casting a
warm cone of light. On the desk's surface is a tidy queue of green-
bubble chat envelopes arriving from the left edge and being sorted by a
dispassionate brass-gloved hand into three bronze bins on the right
(sent / received / queued). Above the desk, a small mechanical
clockwork wheel turns slowly — the schedule. A faint teal steam-vent
puff rises from one corner where a pipe meets the desk.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, desk centered, two lamps symmetrically placed flanking
  the central sorting area
- Incoming queue flows from left, sorted bins on right
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- Clockwork wheel small but visible above the desk

## Palette
- Warm brass and honey gold (desk, bins, lamps)
- Whatsapp green (envelopes)
- Warm parchment cream (lamp shades, light pools)
- Cool teal (steam vent)
- Brass with patina — old, well-used, reliable

## Mood
Orderly deskwork, the post room at midnight, messages flowing through.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
