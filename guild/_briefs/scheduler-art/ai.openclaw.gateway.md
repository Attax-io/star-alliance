# Scheduler Art Brief — ai.openclaw.gateway

Job id: `ai.openclaw.gateway`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A heavy wrought-iron gateway set into a stone arch, blocking a wide
flowing river of luminous data-streams. The lock that holds the gate
shut is shaped unmistakably like a great curved claw — three iron
fingers gripping a single hinge-bolt. The river beneath is bright
cyan-blue currents of glowing glyphs and pulsing packets. Through the
iron bars of the gate, a sunrise of warm gold and rose light is rising
over distant mountains — the data is reaching the other side. The claw
locks it in place until the right time.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, gate centered, river flows from foreground into the gate
- Sunrise visible only through the bars, suggesting passage
- Ornate gold runic border framing the tile, dark parchment
  background, pure black outer rim
- Stone pillars of the gate have small carved claw-runes

## Palette
- Cold iron gray-black (gate, claw)
- Bright cyan/teal (data river)
- Sunrise gold, peach, soft rose (the other side)
- Aged stone gray (pillars)
- Cold outside, warm inside — the contrast tells the story

## Mood
Watchful, gating, the moment of holding something back so it can be
released at the right moment.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
