# Scheduler Art Brief — com.whatsapp-mcp.bridge

Job id: `com.whatsapp-mcp.bridge`
Kind: launchd (System Timer)
Group: System Timers

## Subject
A rope bridge spanning a chasm between two castle walls. The bridge's
"ropes" are strings of green-bubble chat envelopes linked end-to-end
(whatsapp-green coloring), the "planks" are stacked chat-bubble cards
with tiny ellipsis marks. A swift courier figure on a small galloping
horse is mid-crossing, bolt-fast, an envelope clutched in hand. The
LEFT castle wall has a green chat-bubble banner flying from its tower;
the RIGHT castle wall has a brass cog-banner (the system). The bridge
sways slightly, taut and load-bearing.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, bridge horizontal across the middle, courier centered
- Both castle walls frame the edges of the tile
- Chasm below the bridge visible as a thin strip of dark mist
- Ornate gold runic border, dark parchment background, pure black rim
- Slight motion-blur on the courier

## Palette
- Whatsapp green (chat bubbles, banner, bubbles)
- Warm sandstone (castle walls)
- Brass and iron (cog banner, hardware)
- Deep blue-gray mist (chasm)
- Lively, mid-transit

## Mood
The courier always crossing. Reliable in-flight delivery.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
