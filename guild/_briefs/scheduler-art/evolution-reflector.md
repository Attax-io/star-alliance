# Scheduler Art Brief — evolution-reflector

Job id: `evolution-reflector`
Kind: native (Claude Routine)
Group: Claude Routines

## Subject
An obsidian mirror resting on a stone pedestal at the center of a spiral
chamber. In the mirror's dark reflective surface, a quill made of pale
silver light is writing on its own — the nib scribing faint self-revision
lines into an open ledger that exists only inside the reflection. Behind
the mirror, the spiral chamber coils inward in successive stone arches
(each arch a previous revision), lit by a single hanging lantern of
amber light. A faint orbiting ring of small mirror-shards at the rim of
the frame catches and re-emits the reflection, suggesting the system
observing itself.

## Composition
- 1:1 square, subject centered on the obsidian mirror, ledger-quill
  visible inside it
- Spiral arches receding behind, three or four visible turns
- Ornate gold runic border framing the tile, dark parchment
  background, pure black outer rim
- Lantern hangs slightly off-center top, casting warm pool of light
  on the mirror face

## Palette
- Obsidian black with cool blue highlights (mirror face)
- Warm amber/gold (lantern, quill glow, ledger ink-glow)
- Aged parchment tan and dark leather brown (frame, chamber stone)
- Rich saturated but dark overall — dramatic chiaroscuro

## Mood
Quiet, recursive, a little uncanny. The system watching itself
improve. Self-reflection as ritual, not vanity.

## Style
Fallen-Sword MMORPG style. Fantasy RPG scheduler tile. Dark parchment
background with aged leather texture. Gold runic border. Ornate medieval
frame. Dramatic lighting. Rich saturated colors. 48x48 icon style.
Pure black outer border. No text, no watermarks.
