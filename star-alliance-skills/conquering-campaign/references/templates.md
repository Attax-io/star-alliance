# Templates

Canonical shapes for every artefact a campaign produces. Copy and adapt — don't reinvent these formats per run, the consistency is what makes campaigns auditable.

Templates are grouped by mode. The campaign-plan template has audit and build variants. The findings file / synthesis pair (audit) and phase-plan / risk-sweep pair (build) are mode-specific. Checkpoint registry, vault-log, and the subagent prompt skeleton are shared.
